## Why a Personal Homepage?

In an era of social media profiles and link-in-bio tools, a personal homepage still matters. It's a piece of the web you own completely.

## What We're Building

A lightweight, zero-cost personal site using:

- **Astro** for static site generation
- **Tailwind CSS** for styling
- **GitHub Pages** for deployment

The result is fast, free, and fully under your control.
